Our Mission

COVID became a daily part of our everyday lives. Many authorities require the continued use of rapid antigen tests (RAT), and there are too many choices and too little information available. That's why we created this project.

Learn more about us

A Community Project

Covidrat.info is a community-driven project that aims to help you learn more about any rapid testing kit. All information presented here are collected by community members and updated by our volunteers.
