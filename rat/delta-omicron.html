Our methodologies

We've compiled lists from the European Commission and the Australian Department of Health to create this searchable database for anyone wondering: Can COVID rapid antigen tests reliably detect Omicron or Delta?

Here is how these data got here

Both Australia and the EU have kept a close eye on the effectiveness of rapid tests against Delta and Omicron.The studies were produced by labs hired by the manufacturers themselves, then the collected data were submitted to the officials for review.

[Please read] This is how you should understand the data

Omicron is shown to be less detectable by the average rapid test.
Some manufacturers claim that their rapid tests perform similarly for Omicron/Delta variants.
A green tick on the chart above indicates that the relevant test kit is as good at detecting the Delta/Omicron variant as detecting the non-variant COVID.

No, the manufacturing date does not indicate effectiveness against Omicron or Delta

Yes, Omicron was first detected on 24th Nov 2021. No, this doesn't mean that test kits made after that date will automatically become effective at detecting Omicron.
