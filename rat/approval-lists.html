You can quickly verify the marketing claims of a rapid test using this tool

Enter the brand or manufacturer on the box and quickly find out if any trusted Government agencies have officially recognized this particular brand of COVID rapid antigen test.

CE/ISO certifications do not make a rapid test officially recognized

The EU has a set of strict lab requirements for rapid tests to be listed in the Common List of COVID-19 Rapid Antigen Tests, agreed by the Health Security Committee.
Getting CE/ISO certified requires a different set of qualifications, and these are applicable to general goods, not just COVID tests.

Are we going to include other lists?

There are lists of rapid tests created by other countries such as Korea, Malaysia, etc. The data on here are (currently) collected manually and we are looking to more automation before including more official lists.
